What is Babes Helpin' Babes?

Babes Helpin' Babes is an employee-driven resource group that exists to uplift the voices of women and BIPOC, challenge norms and create meaningful change at Signal Theory.

Why is voting essential to the BHB mission?

Voting is imperative to furthering diversity, equity & inclusion within our country. As election results typically reflect opinions of smaller groups, we're encouraging the masses to get to the polls so results more accurately embody the diverse opinions of all citizens.
